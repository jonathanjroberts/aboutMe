Work Experience

American Composites Manufacturers Association

The American Composites Manufacturers Association (ACMA) is a non profit trade association that is dedicated to expanding the use of composites materials including glass-fiber and carbon-fiber in manufactured products. The most frequent products the association advocates for include automotive vehicles, bridge decking, corrosive resistant tanks and bathroom vanities.

At ACMA I was responsible for the growth initiative committees which included:

  • Architectural Division
  • Automotive Composites Alliance
  • Corrosion Control Division
  • Fiberglass Grating Manufacturers Council
  • FRP-Rebar Manufacturers Council
  • Green Composites Council
  • High Performance Council
  • International Cast Polymer Alliance
  • Pultrusion Industry Council
  • Press Molders Council
  • Transportation Structures Council
  • Utility & Communications Structures Council

Each committee has a volunteer chair who was responsible for accomplishing the goals and projects that committee members agreed to. I worked with each chair to complete the logistical tasks such as meeting prep and follow-up, minutes, project management and fundraising.
