About Me

I enjoy outdoor activities including tennis, hiking, rock climbing, biking, waterparks, and basketball. I also enjoy spending time with family playing games and making family movies.

My claim to fame is I climbed a 20,000 foot mountain in Bolivia called Huayna Potosi located on the outskirts of La Paz. The climb was one of the most difficult physical activities I have ever done. During the climb I got altitude sickness, but I wasn't going to let that stop me.

I also enjoy car trips. I have been to every state in the United States except Hawaii because there is no road to get there. The longest car trip I was on lasted 4 weeks and traveled through 37 states.

In addition to car trips, I enjoy bus travel. I have traveled through Latin American mostly by bus. In Brazil I took a 36 hour bus ride from Belem to Joao Passoa. I have also traveled by bus from La Paz, Bolivia to Quito, Ecuador and from Panama City, Panama to San Salvador, El Salvador.
